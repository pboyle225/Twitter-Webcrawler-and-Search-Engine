VMware Sr MTS QE in Palo Alto California United States

Posting Title

Sr MTS QE

Job Description

This is a Sr. MTS level position with ISBU- EVO RACK Product Engineering Team. EVO RACK team comprises highly talented engineering team with proven track records in developing datacenter mission critical products. EVO RACK is a Converged & Hyper Converged Infrastructure solution that enables our customers enabling to reduce costs and provide time-to-value to their customers with a pre-integrated and optimized bundle of hardware and software across networking, compute servers, and storage typically combined with virtualization, management, and other software (DR, load balancing, etc.). This is an exciting area to work and a fast-paced work culture. If developing cutting-edge products that solve large real world distributed systems problems and delivering them on an aggressive schedule motivates you, then this job is for you.

Role: As part of the VMware SDDC Emerging Solutions - Product Engineering team, you will work with the team of strong engineers building products in this area. As a member of the team you will be working on Build and release engineering, Continuous Integration testing of a scalable, data center-wide distributed management system software to deliver on the promise of automated provisioning, deployment, and operations of the data center infrastructure. You will closely work with developers, QEs and PMs to deliver quality releases. Your responsibilities will include but not be limited to:

  • Build and release engineering.

  • Design, implement and execute test plan against the core components of vRACK/EVO RACK

  • Automate functional and end to end test cases using Java and python and integrate in to Continuous Integration Framework

  • Understand and integrate third party products and components to work seamlessly with the software

  • Ensure that proposed and existing architectures are aligned with organizational goals and objectives

  • Find product defects and follow up to closure

Develop innovate solutions in testing Converged and Hyper Converged Infrastructure domain. Position Requirements

  • University degree in the field of computer science/engineering or information systems (Master’s degree preferred).

  • 8+ years’ work experience with 6+ years of System Software development test experience.

  • Proven experience in the Hardware management of servers, storage appliances, and network switches

  • Proven experience in VMware technologies vSphere, vCenter, NSX etc.

  • Experience in OpenStack software suite and integration of orchestration and resource provisioning layers

  • Network architecture including L2 and L3 protocols for routing, multi-link aggregation, QoS, and security

  • System software development with exposure to drivers, Linux kernel

  • Exposure to distributed software systems including use of utilities such as Apache Zookeeper, Cassandra, Cages, and related projects highly desirable

  • Hand-on technical experience in C++, Python, J2EE and Scripting.

  • Experience in implementing integrations using SOAP, REST, JSON etc.

  • Exceptional analytical, conceptual, and problem-solving abilities.

  • Able to prioritize and execute tasks in a high-pressure environment.

  • Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.

  • Motivated and self-driving personnel.

  • Superior written and oral communication skills.

  • Strong interpersonal and consultative skills.

Why work for our Division

VMware’s world-class, award-winning R&D team is comprised of thousands of top-notch computer scientists and software engineers that are transforming computing through virtualization. Members of the R&D team voice their creative ideas and watch them become initiatives, participate in exciting short-term and long-term products on the VMware roadmap, initiate advanced research projects, and/or write and test code that ships. At VMware, our engineers continue to learn and grow by working with top professionals from around the world and recent graduates of top universities and by taking advantage of our extensive internal training opportunities and generous external education assistance program. R&D team members work on the latest computing equipment and have access to the ACM and IEEE libraries. VMware has R&D offices in: Silicon Valley; Burlington, MA; Cambridge, MA; Broomfield, CO; Sofia, Bulgaria; Aarhus, Denmark; London, UK; Herzliya, Israel; Bangalore, India; and Beijing, China.
